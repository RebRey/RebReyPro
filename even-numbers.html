Even and Odd Number Calculators

Knowing if a number is odd or even is very easy. You just have to divide the number by two.

Even Numbers: A number which is divisible by 2 and generates a remainder of 0. The answer will be a whole number.

Odd Numbers: A number which is not divisible by 2 and always generates a remainder of 1. The answer will be a decimal.
